About Our Desi Eggs

Kaushalya Desi Eggs brings you the purest, most nutritious eggs — straight from our semi-free-range Gavran poultry farm. Our hens roam freely across 2 acres of natural farmland, feeding on organic, chemical-free food. Every egg is rich in taste, freshness, and Desi tradition, carefully handled for your health and trust.

Our Mission

We aim to deliver India’s healthiest Desi eggs — raised naturally, cared for with love, and distributed with honesty. Our dream is to make organic food accessible to everyone while supporting sustainable farming in rural communities.
